About

"Meet Dr. Divya Agrawal"

Dr. Divya Agrawal, located in Mahmoorganj, Varanasi, is a leading Gynecologist, Obstetrician and Infertility Specialist with extensive experience in the respective fields. She provides comprehensive pre- and post-natal care for pregnant women, ensuring the health of both mother and child. Specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the vagina, ovaries, uterus, and breasts, Dr. Agrawal's clinic is equipped with advanced medical technologies. Services include laparoscopy & hysteroscopy and IVF - ICSI and Infertility treatment, offering personalized and effective healthcare solutions for women.
